Crop Nutritional Global Ontology (CNGO) is a Minimum Information to Reference an External Ontology Term (MIREOT) based ontology that anables access, annotation, and comparison of nutritional data for underutilised crops. the ontology defelopment involves the selection of relavant ontology terms in well extablished ontologies without importing the entire ontology hierarchies. The ontology re-used Open Biological and Biomedical  (OBO) Foundry Ontologies including Basic Formal Ontology (BFO) as the upper level ontology. The adoption of a MIREOT-based strategy provides a practical importance of reusing relevant ontologies terms from well established ontologies without importing complete ontologies, thereby minimising computational overhead while preserving semantic consistency.  This enables semantic integration and comparison of  crop trait datasets from diverse sources.
